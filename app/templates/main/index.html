Purpose of our build

The purpose of our project was to create a place where people who are bored out of their minds by quarantine can use. We compiled a list of gems of movies (top rated on imdb) for people to watch. We created a chat application that lets them talk to others. And we created a page filled with blogs, guiding people in these troubled times.
